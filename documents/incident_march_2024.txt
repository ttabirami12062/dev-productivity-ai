INCIDENT REPORT - MARCH 2024

Date: March 15, 2024
Severity: High
Duration: 6 hours

Summary:
The payment service went down for 6 hours causing 30% revenue loss.

Root Cause:
PR review bottleneck. One senior developer was reviewing all PRs alone.
Three critical bug fix PRs sat unreviewed for 48 hours before merging.
A breaking change slipped through due to rushed review.

Warning Signs That Were Missed:
- PR review time increased from 12 hours to 48 hours over 2 weeks
- One developer had 80% of all review assignments
- Code churn was high in the payment module for 3 consecutive weeks

Resolution:
- Redistributed PR reviews across 3 developers
- Set maximum review time of 24 hours
- Added required two reviewer rule for payment module

Lessons Learned:
When average PR review time exceeds 36 hours and one person handles
over 70% of reviews, delivery risk is critical. Redistribute immediately.
